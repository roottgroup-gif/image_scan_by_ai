Most images are analyzed within 2-3 seconds. Processing time may vary based on image size, complexity, and current server load. Our system is optimized for real-time analysis.

Technical Questions

How does the detection technology work?

Our system uses a hybrid approach combining:

  • Deep Learning: ResNet50 neural network for pattern recognition
  • Texture Analysis: Local Binary Patterns and edge detection
  • Frequency Analysis: FFT-based spectral examination
  • Compression Forensics: JPEG artifact detection
  • Statistical Analysis: Pixel pattern recognition

What are detection indicators?

Detection indicators are specific visual or technical patterns that suggest AI generation. These include unusual texture patterns, frequency anomalies, compression artifacts, and statistical irregularities. Each indicator is rated as Strong, Moderate, or Weak based on its significance.

Can the system be fooled?

While our detection rate is very high (96.8%), no detection system is 100% perfect. We continuously update our algorithms to stay ahead of new AI generation techniques. For critical applications, we recommend using multiple verification methods.

Do you offer an API?

Yes, we offer API access for developers and businesses who need to integrate AI detection into their applications. Contact us at api@rootgroup.tech for more information about our API plans and pricing.

Privacy & Security

Is my data secure and private?

Yes, absolutely. Your images are processed securely and are never permanently stored on our servers. All processing happens in real-time and files are immediately deleted after analysis. We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission.

Do you store uploaded images?

No, we do not store uploaded images. Images are temporarily processed in memory and immediately deleted once the analysis is complete. This ensures complete privacy and security of your data.

What about GDPR compliance?

We are fully GDPR compliant. Since we don't store personal data or images, there's minimal data processing involved. Our service processes images locally and temporarily for analysis purposes only.

Can I use this for commercial purposes?

Yes, our service can be used for commercial purposes including content verification, publishing, social media platforms, and more. For high-volume commercial use, please contact us about our enterprise solutions.
